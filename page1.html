THE BURDEN OF THIS MANDATE

“He must free them because he had paid in full for their crimes"

WOREM stands for Word Of Restoration Evangelistic Ministries. This commission was founded by God with the mandate of reaching out to the unreached where none is saying restore and restoring them to their full destinies in Christ Jesus.  

The Holy Spirit specifically told me that there are people that have been robbed, spoiled, snared in holes, hidden in prison houses; they are for a prey, and none delivereth; for a spoil, and none saith, Restore. But then He has given me the word of restoration to counter the devil’s mission over them and restore them to their full potential in Christ. He told me to do the work of evangelism more and make full proof of my ministry. 


In one of my revelations, the Lord brought me to the midst of a crowd of young people grouped into two. The first group of these young people was chained and kept under torture as they wept bitterly, while the other group was already languishing in torment in the midst of the kindled fire.

While I was standing between the two groups negotiating their release with their captor who was condemning and torturing them for the crime they committed. Their captor kept saying no they are guilty and deserve to join the first group. But suddenly, a man of God whom I know to be my friend appeared on the scene and replied the captor that, 

“He must free them because he had paid in full for their crimes. I woke up with passion from my bed with a fresh burden and commitment for evangelize especially to the youth. As a matter of fact, I made a commitment from then that I will be going out for Evangelism at two different period of the day i.e. Morning and Evening.

A BRIEF HISTORY OF THE PASTOR

Anayochukwu John Nwachukwu (AJN) Odiomume began his journey with the LORD Jesus Christ on Sunday 20th December 1996 and got baptized by immersion on Saturday 22nd August 1998. He gave his life to Christ at the age of 15 and ever since then had dedicated his life as a full time minister of the gospel serving in the vineyard of God. He is a humble, dynamic and experienced young minister of the gospel

He is an Intercessor, a preacher, a pastor, an author and a teacher of the undiluted word of God. He started ministry as a youth minister and a fervent labourer in God’s vineyard and has continued with it for more than 22years. Since his teenage, he has been preaching the gospel of our Lord and Savior, Jesus Christ as his major occupation.

 In all these years, God has used him to effect changes in the lives of youths, teenagers and children. At present, God is still using him to make greater impacts in the lives of young people globally. He is the founder of Word of Restoration Evangelistic Ministries (WOREM INT’L).

He hails from Umuechi, Mgbidi Town of Oru West LGA in Imo State, Nigeria. He is a Competent, Conference Speaker. He is available for teens/youth conference, Revival programmes, Youth / Single Seminars, Crusades, Workshop, etc. He is a gift and a blessing to his generation.
