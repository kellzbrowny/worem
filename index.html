MISSION  

“Restoring the people robbed, spoiled, snared in holes and hidden in prison house; where none saith, Restore”-Isaiah 42:22

VISION  

To reconcile people back to Christ, restore them to their full potentials in Christ Jesus and help them find their place in life via the word of God 2ND Corinthians 5:18-19.

THE BURDEN OF THIS MANDATE

WOREM stands for Word Of Restoration Evangelistic Ministries. This commission was founded by God with the mandate of reaching out to the unreached where none is saying restore and restoring them to their full destinies in Christ Jesus.
